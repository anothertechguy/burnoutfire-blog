# Quality Benchmarks

## Overview

This document defines quality standards for BurnoutFIRE content. All content must meet these benchmarks before publication.

## Content Quality Metrics

### Word Count
- **Pillar Posts**: 2,500+ words
- **Supporting Articles**: 1,500-2,500 words
- **Tactical Posts**: 800-1,200 words
- **Evergreen Guides**: 2,000+ words

### Readability
- **Target**: 60-80 Flesch Reading Ease
- **Grade Level**: 8th-10th grade
- **Sentence Length**: Average 15-20 words
- **Paragraph Length**: 3-4 sentences

### Content Depth
- **Minimum Score**: 75/100
- **Factors**: Word count, heading structure, section depth
- **Target**: Exceed competitor average by 20%+

### Uniqueness
- **Minimum Score**: 80/100
- **Measurement**: Comparison vs. top 10 competitors
- **Target**: Unique angle or deeper coverage

### Actionability
- **Minimum Score**: 70/100
- **Requirements**: Clear takeaways, actionable steps
- **Target**: Readers know what to do next

### Research Quality
- **Minimum Score**: 80/100
- **Requirements**: Research-backed claims or clearly labeled opinions
- **Target**: All claims supported or qualified

---

## SEO Quality Metrics

### Keyword Usage
- **Density**: 1-3% (natural, not stuffed)
- **Title**: Include primary keyword
- **First Paragraph**: Include keyword naturally
- **Headings**: Use in at least one H2/H3

### Meta Tags
- **Title**: 50-60 characters
- **Description**: 150-160 characters
- **Keywords**: Include primary + 2-3 related

### Structure
- **Headings**: Minimum 5 H2/H3 headings
- **Internal Links**: 3 per 1,000 words
- **External Links**: 2+ authoritative sources
- **Schema**: BlogPosting schema markup

---

## Competitive Benchmarks

### Word Count
- **Target**: Above competitor average
- **Minimum**: 20% more than average
- **Ideal**: 50%+ more for pillar posts

### Structure
- **Target**: Better than competitors
- **Requirements**: More organized, clearer sections
- **Measurement**: Heading hierarchy, section depth

### Depth
- **Target**: Deeper than competitors
- **Requirements**: More comprehensive coverage
- **Measurement**: Topics covered, detail level

### Angle
- **Target**: Unique perspective
- **Requirements**: Different angle or deeper analysis
- **Measurement**: Uniqueness score vs. competitors

---

## Quality Scoring Process

### Pre-Publication Checklist
- [ ] Word count meets target
- [ ] Readability score 60-80
- [ ] Content depth score ≥75
- [ ] Uniqueness score ≥80
- [ ] Actionability score ≥70
- [ ] Research score ≥80
- [ ] SEO optimized (keywords, meta tags)
- [ ] Internal links added (3 per 1,000 words)
- [ ] External links added (2+ authoritative)
- [ ] Schema markup validated
- [ ] Competitive advantage verified

### Scoring Method
1. **Automated**: Use quality scoring tools
2. **Manual Review**: Editor review for tone, uniqueness
3. **Competitive Check**: Compare to top 10 competitors
4. **Final Approval**: Must pass all benchmarks

## Continuous Improvement

### Track Performance
- **Rankings**: Monitor keyword rankings
- **Traffic**: Track organic traffic
- **Engagement**: Monitor time on page, bounce rate
- **Conversions**: Track email signups, affiliate clicks

### Refine Benchmarks
- **Monthly**: Review performance data
- **Quarterly**: Adjust benchmarks based on results
- **Annually**: Comprehensive benchmark review

## Notes

Quality benchmarks ensure all content meets high standards and beats competitors. These benchmarks should be reviewed and updated based on performance data and competitive analysis.
